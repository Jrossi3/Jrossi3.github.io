Why Swimming?

Health Benefits

Swimming keeps your heart rate up but takes some of the impact stress off your body. It builds endurance, muscle strength and cardiovascular fitness helps maintain a healthy weight, healthy heart and lungs tones muscles and builds strength provides an all-over body workout, as nearly all of your muscles are used during swimming. It is a resistance-training exercise and is fantastic for toning shoulders, arms, backs, and abs and it's easy to do!

Life Skill

Swimming is a life skill. Learning it at a young age will allow your children to become more atheletic at a younger age. Kids can learn as early as 6 months old in proper conditions. It has the potential to save lives and gives your children the safety and comfort of going into an area of water where they are unable to stand.
